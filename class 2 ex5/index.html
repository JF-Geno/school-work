Red panda

The red panda, also known as the lesser panda, is a small mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. It has dense reddish-brown fur with a black belly and legs, white-lined ears, a mostly white muzzle and a ringed tail.Wikipedia

  • Lifespan: 9-13 years
  • Scientific name:Ailurus fulgens
  • Mass3.7 – 6.2 kg (Male), 3 – 6 kg (Female)
  • Length58 cm (Adult)

PersonalityRed pandas are territorial and solitary except during the mating season. They are crepuscular and nocturnal, spending the day sleeping in trees and using the night to mark territory with urine and musk and to seek food. They clean themselves, much like cats, and communicate using twittering sounds and whistles.
